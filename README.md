## A little graphic library providing matrix transformations

![image](https://github.com/Zarathustra4/GraphicsLib/assets/68013193/80a14e32-ee93-4675-bfea-d04313ae17c4)


To use a transformation press the button from 1 to 6:
1) Scaling;
2) Rotate around Y and after around X axes;
3) Rotate around X and after around Y axes;
4) Rotate around body's edge;
5) Rotate around body's diagonal;
6) Mirror the body at it's face;
